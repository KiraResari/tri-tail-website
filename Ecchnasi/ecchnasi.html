Ecchnasi is a simple yet highly tactical board game for two players, similar in nature to chess and checkers. Playing with 18 pieces per player on an 9x9 grid, each player tries to cleverly maneuver their wolves in order to break through the other pack, and conquer a safe spot in their home row.
