In the primal forests of Nakkitam, there are two packs of animals, competing for dominance. Now the time has come to find out who is worthy to become Ecchnasi, the great pack leader, destined to lead both packs.

Ecchnasi is a simple yet highly tactical board game for two players, similar in nature to chess and checkers. Playing with 18 pieces per player on an 9x9 grid, each player tries to cleverly maneuver their pieces in order to break through the other pack, and conquer a safe spot at the far side of the field.
