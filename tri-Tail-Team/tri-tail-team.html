Kira Resari

The Travelling Fox

Kira has travelled all around the world in search of inspiration. He has been working on the Chronicles of Ceal since school, and never lost sight of his dream.

He originally studied Game Design, and is presently working as an Apps Developer, dedicating his spare time to the pursuit of his dream.
