The long-overdue website update!

January 24, 2025 | by Kira Resari

After procrastinating for almost a decade, I am now finally getting around to reworking the tri-Tail, Ceal and Ecchnasi websites. I am doing this one page at a time, so please excuse me if the website is going to look just a little bit inconsistent during that time.

One of the main points I want to address with this update is making the website easier to maintain, so I won't end up not updating it for so long. I did create the old website with little knowledge of html, and pretty much relied on an authoring software (I think it was Magix Web Designer) to translate what I designed on on a GUI into working html code, and let me tell you, that code was not pretty. That was one of the main reasons why I didn't update the website this long.

By contrast, this new website is written exclusively in html from the start, with the aid of my good friend Chatty (aka ChatGPT), whom I by now think of as something akin to a digital Puky.

As a result, the new website will look a lot more streamlined. I'll probably lose some of the more fancy design elements (like the interactive world map under Ceal/World) along the way, but I'll try to preserve as much as I can.

I'm also using this opportunity to clear the news history, since most of the entries there have long since been outdated anyway, so here's to a fresh start!

Also, one little announcement: Having brought the Travelling Fox Blog to an official close last year, I am now re-dedicating myself to the Chronicles of Ceal once again. As such, the gears have already been put in motion, and you can expect great things for the Chronicles of Ceal in the not-so-distant future. Until then, stay tuned, and be of great cheer! =^,^=
